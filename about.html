About Me

Hello! Welcome to my blog site. I began this site to journal about my hobbies. I enjoy baking all kinds of desserts and sharing them with my friends. I love creating origami and pop-up art.

I volunteer at the East Rochester Library through the Literacy Rochester Organization. I troubleshoot and provide technical solutions to others, and teach folks computer skills. I even provide help with improving resumes. I am proudly a member of Pinnacle Toastmasters in Pittsford New York. I am working on improving my confidence and public-speaking skills.

For my profession, I am currently pursuing a career as a full-stack software engineer.

Thank you for visiting the site!
